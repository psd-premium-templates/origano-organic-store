Overview

Origano - Organic Store PSD Template designed specifically for Organic Store, Dairy Farm, Organic Farming, Farm, Organic Food, Organic Food Shop, Dairy Farm, Bakery Shop and many other services.

It's loaded with   home pages. PSD Design included 38 well-organized, editable psd files. The Template is based on 1170px Uikit Grid, it is clean, fully customizable, responsive & easy to use.
